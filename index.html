INSITE: Integrating scRNAseq for Tcell Engineering

INSITE provides comprehensive analysis tools for T-cell single-cell RNA sequencing data, with focus on perturbation effects and cellular responses. Use the navigation menu to explore datasets, predict perturbation effects, or optimize gene targets for desired outcomes.
